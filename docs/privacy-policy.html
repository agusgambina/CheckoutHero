Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

CheckoutHero ("the App") is a local-only shopping list application for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the App accesses and how it is used.

No Data Collection

CheckoutHero does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The App makes no network requests of its own. All data you enter — shopping lists, items, prices — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and never leaves it.

Apple Reminders

If you choose to import reminders, the App requests access to Apple Reminders on your device. This access is used solely to read reminder titles and import them as shopping items. Reminder data is processed entirely on-device and is never sent anywhere.

Sign In with Apple

If you sign in with Apple, your Apple User ID and display name (if provided) are stored locally in your device's Keychain. This information is never transmitted to any server. Sign In with Apple is entirely optional; the App is fully functional without it.

In-App Purchases (Tip Jar)

Optional tip purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. CheckoutHero does not receive or store any payment information. Apple's Privacy Policy governs all purchase transactions.

Analytics & Advertising

The App contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Children's Privacy

CheckoutHero does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be published at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
