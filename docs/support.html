Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a shopping list?

Tap the + button in the top-right corner of the Shopping Lists screen to create a new list. Give it a name and start adding items.

How do I add items to a list?

Open a shopping list, then tap the + button to add a new item. You can set a name, quantity, and price for each item.

How do I import from Apple Reminders?

Go to Settings → Import. Select the destination shopping list, choose a Reminders list, then select which reminders to import as items. The app will request access to your Reminders when you first use this feature.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account. Scroll down and tap Delete Account. Confirm the action in the dialog that appears. This permanently removes your Apple account data (User ID and name) from your device's Keychain. Your shopping list data (stored via SwiftData on-device) is not affected and can be deleted separately through iOS Settings → CheckoutHero → Delete App.

What data does CheckoutHero store?

All data is stored locally on your device. The app makes no network requests and does not send any data to external servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I sign out of my Apple account?

Go to Settings → Account and tap Sign Out. Your shopping list data is unaffected.

The app is not working as expected

Try the following steps:

  • Force-quit the app and reopen it.
  • Restart your device.
  • Make sure you are running the latest version of CheckoutHero from the App Store.
  • If the problem persists, contact us using the email below.
